We are living through a transformation unlike anything we have seen in generations. AI is not just another tool. It is a partner we can dance with.

But here is what that really means: when everyone can dance with machines, the human leading the dance becomes everything. Your taste. Your judgment. Your questions. What you choose to make and why you choose to make it.

Sparks With Soul is where those questions live. It is a place for the moments, signals, and ideas that reveal where culture and business are headed — and what it means to stay irreducibly human while they do.

“While machines compute, humans connect.”

Who is Tina Sharkey?

01

The Builder

Tina spent three decades architecting human connection at planetary scale. AOL. iVillage. BabyCenter. Brandless. She coined the term “social media” before it was a category. Each platform taught her the same lesson: when people feel seen and connected, they ignite sparks that travel beyond borders.

AOL iVILLAGE BABYCENTER BRANDLESS
02

The Teacher

Today Tina is a Lecturer at USC’s Jimmy Iovine and Andre Young Academy, where she directs the Innovation Quest and leads a Gen AI grant. From the classroom to the boardroom, she advises Fortune 500 companies and growth startups on strategy, storytelling, and what comes next in a world reshaped by AI.

USC Iovine & Young Academy Fortune 500 Advisor Gen AI Grant Director
03

The Patient

Last summer, a splitting headache that wouldn’t fade. Five days later, an ER at UCSF. A brain tumor the size of a small avocado. Inoperable, they said. Tina told her doctor: you handle the science, I’ll handle the soul. Ten days later, the tumor was gone — and a framework for living remained.

“Fear is a wasted emotion. Soul is the most powerful intelligence we have.”
